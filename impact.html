His impact

Today, Junji Ito still creates manga and has reached worldwide fame. Some of his works have been mentioned in popular media today, such as inspiration for an episode of Cartoon Network's Steven Universe. Ito's series Tomie has also seen several movie adaptations, as the case with Uzumaki as well. With Uzumaki getting an anime adaptation on Adult Swim.

Junji Ito's works have also inspired severla cosplayers and makeup artists into turing his art into reality.
