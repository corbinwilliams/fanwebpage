Junji Ito

Junji Ito, born July 31st, 1963, is a Japanese manga writer responsable for many scary stories. He is most known for creating the manga series Uzumaki, Tomie, the anthalogies Smahsed, and Shiver. Making his manga debut in 1983, Ito is most known for his horrifying stories that stick in the mind for days on end.
